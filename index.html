Mutton karahi

mutton karahi is one of my favourite food.here is a recipe of mutton karahi.

Ingredients

  • 500gm of mutton meat
  • 1 packet of mutton karahi masla
  • 100gm of yougurt
  • 50 to 100gm of tomato
  • 100gm of oil
  • different type of masala

Method

  1. put oil on heat
  2. add tomatoes
  3. add meat
  4. add masala
  5. add packet of masala

final step

  • after all this step
  • wait 20 to 25 minutes
  • your delicious karahi is ready
